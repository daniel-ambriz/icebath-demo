What is the Cold Plunge?

Cold Water Immersion (Cold Plunge) involves immersion of the body into water at temperatures less than 16°C / 60°F.

The benefits of doing the Cold Plunge are numerous, especially if regularly plunging in cold temperatures for short periods of time. In the past 10+ years, more and more athletes have been incorporating the Cold Plunge into their regimen. Some have attributed their success to it.

In this way, Cold Plunge therapy continues to gain popularity and advocates from athletes and people with physical ailments to entrepeneurs and professionals alike.
